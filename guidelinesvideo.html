Video Guidelines for RIISE 2022


  • » Please submit your poster videos by uploading them here: https://forms.gle/LpksAriUwQkTZCsT8
  • » Video should be shot in Landscape Mode.
  • » The duration of video should be 6-8 minutes.
  • » Keep the highest quality for shooting with an aspect ratio of 16:9.
  • » Send the files via email as attachment to Google Drive. (This way we will receive uncompressed video, which gets compressed when shared with whatsApp).
  • » Try using a tripod to get good camera stabilisation.
  • » Shoot in a well lit up area.
  • » If you are recording a video in screen record mode, make sure your face is also visible (at least 20% of the screen size).
  • » While speaking about a subject, please keep in mind to avoid background noises.
  • » If possible use a lapel mic for voice recording.
  • » The file name should be prefixed with the poster code that you received from the supporting person for your primary theme. The names are listed below again for your reference.
    • 1. Artificial Intelligence - Priti Patwal
    • 2. Core Research - Risha Lal
    • 3. Cyber Physical Systems - Sanjna Khosla
    • 4. Design - Binu Ann Joseph
    • 5. Healthcare - Shipra Jain
    • 6. Lab to Market/ Start-ups - Vibhor Jain
    • 7. Sustainability - Binoj Baby
    • 8. Technology as a Public Good - Abhijeet Mishra
  • » Please submit the videos based on the final posters by Wednesday, 30-March-2022.

For any other queries, please reach us at riise@iiitd.ac.in .

Looking forward to having you all in the event. Hope you all will enjoy the sessions and learn various concepts.
